General Help (Vendors & Suppliers)

  • How do I register as a Supplier or Vendor?
    On the homepage, click on "Signup" and choose your role. Fill in the registration form and submit.
  • How do I upload materials or products?
    After login, go to your dashboard. Fill the material form with image, name, quantity, unit, and price, then click “Upload”.
  • How do I update or delete a product?
    Each product you upload will be shown in your dashboard with Edit and Delete options.
  • What if I entered wrong information?
    Use the Edit button on the product card to update it.
  • Forgot Password?
    Click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to reset it.
  • Need more help?
    Email us at support@saathibazar.in
